What is 24 hour time format?


It is the same as Military Time. There is no AM or PM, just time expressed from 00:00 to 23:59.

Here is a table showing the various hours of the day in Military Time or 24 hour time format:

MIDNIGHT = 0 hours
1 AM = 1
2 AM = 2
3 AM = 3
4 AM = 4
5 AM = 5
6 AM = 6
7 AM = 7
8 AM = 8
9 AM = 9
10 AM = 10
11 AM = 11

NOON = 12
1 PM = 13
2 PM = 14
3 PM = 15
4 PM = 16
5 PM = 17
6 PM = 18
7 PM = 19
8 PM = 20
9 PM = 21
10 PM = 22
11 PM = 23 hours

The only thing that changes is the hour (not the minute).
