Mind group:1,5,7

The 1-5-7s are always thinking. They are often well educated, enjoy traveling, and
need to use their high energy in a healthy way, or that same energy can be destructive.

Being surrounded by nature and doing physical exercise are two great ways for these
numbers to get out of their heads and enjoy life more. It is easy for the 1-5-7 numbers
to fall into the habit of dissecting their partners as though they were science projects.
If they marry one of these Natural Match Numbers, they will have a partner of like
mind, who will understand—and it won’t be a problem.
